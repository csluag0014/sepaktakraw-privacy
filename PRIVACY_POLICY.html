Privacy Policy for Sepak Takraw Scoring App

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sepak Takraw Scoring App ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game History: Match results, scores, team names, and game statistics
  • Team Names: Custom team names you enter
  • Game Settings: Your game preferences and settings
Important: All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect anonymous usage analytics through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app. This information is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

How We Use Your Information

  • Local Storage: Game history and settings are stored on your device to provide you with access to past matches and preferences
  • App Functionality: Your data is used solely to provide scoring and game management features within the app
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data helps us understand how the app is used and improve user experience

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. All game data remains on your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for:

  • Keeping your device secure
  • Backing up your data if needed (through your device's backup system)
  • Protecting your device from unauthorized access

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can clear game history at any time through the app settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • Export: You can export and share match results using the app's built-in sharing features

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app can be used by anyone, but we do not collect any personal data that would require parental consent.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
